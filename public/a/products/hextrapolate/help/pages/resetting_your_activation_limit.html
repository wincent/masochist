Resetting your activation limit

If you exceed your activation limit, contact me via email to support@wincent.com and I will reset your limit. Please provide your contact details, registration email address, and product name so that I can locate your license.

Once your limit has been reset you may then delete your old activation certificate and obtain a new one.

To delete your old activation certificate:

  1. Using the Finder, go to your home directory and open the Library folder
  2. Inside the Library folder, open the Preferences folder
  3. Drag the file com.wincent.Hextrapolate.activation.certificate.plist from the Preferences folder to the Trash.

The next time you launch Hextrapolate it will show a dialog offering to perform activation and obtain a new certificate for you.
