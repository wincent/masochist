Force Quitting Hextrapolate

If Hextrapolate stops responding you may not be able to quit it by the usual means. You may have to do a “force quit”. To do this you can use any of the standard methods for force quitting applications on Mac OS X:

  • Hold down the Option and Command keys and press Escape: the system should display a dialog listing all running applications; you can then select Hextrapolate from the list and press “Force Quit”.
  • Bring up the Hextrapolate Dock menu by clicking and holding on the Hextrapolate icon in the Dock and select “Force Quit”.

If Hextrapolate stops responding please consider filing a bug report.
