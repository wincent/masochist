    Hello I've just created this "Lounge" forum for general discussion. Although it's described as a forum for discussion of "anything and everything", there are some rules. Namely:

    keep things legal: that means no warez, porn, any content that wouldn't be suitable for minors, and anything that could be considered illegal in my country or yours. don't break any NDAs (Non-Disclosure Agreements): as it would apply here, that basically means that if you're going to discuss any forthcoming Apple product releases, do so using only publicly available information that Apple itself has made available to the general public. use common sense: I'm not going to spend my days and nights patrolling this forum, so I'm basically trusting you to keep things nice.

    Bearing these basic guidelines in mind, enjoy!
