UBB.threads version 7 upgrade

  1. Greg Hurrell 2006-10-06T09:51:02Z

    The forums have now been updated to UBB.threads version 7. Please let me know if you see any problems or would like to suggest an improvement.

    There a number of new features available. One of the significant changes is that the new version no longer has "Subscriptions" (for receiving email notifications of when posts are made to forums). If you wish to continue receiving notification emails you'll need to log in and click on "My Stuff", "My Watch Lists" and configure which forums you'd like to watch and how you'd like to be notified.

    There's also an RSS feed available (currently broken; the problem has been reported to the UBB.threads team and I am hoping for a quick fix):

    feed://wincent.com/a/support/forums/cache/global_rss1.xml
