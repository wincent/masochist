  2. Greg Hurrell 2010-12-08T21:19:40Z

    At the moment the transfer is not automatic. See this recent forum topic for more.

    It probably never will be entirely automatic, due to the possibility of errors, but there are tickets in the issue tracker (for example, ticket #459) which talk about ways to streamline the process beyond the existing "Transfer cover to iTunes" menu item.

    One of the ideas for streamlining is that instead of having to do this for each individual cover, you would be able to see a gallery of all covers and easily select multiple ones that you want to transfer over in one hit.
