    You could try quitting Synergy, temporarily moving it's preferences out of the way, and restarting it. You could move the prefs with a command like:

    $ mv ~/Library/Preferences/org.wincent.Synergy.plist ~/Desktop/

    Just to rule out some kind of prefs file corruption.
