  4. Greg Hurrell 2005-02-06T05:56:52Z

    Thanks for the clarification. I've reworded the summary to make things clearer (at least in my mind).

    Ok, the thing about the generic CD icon on the desktop, that's a Finder issue, so it's outside the scope of Synergy.

    As for the icon appearing in the floater and not the album cover, it definitely should work, provided that you have entered adequate identifying information for the tracks in iTunes. Have you tried it with different CDs? Do they have detailed title/artist/album information entered?

    The other thing to remember is that download art doesn't appear instantly. It may take some time to appear. Synergy spreads out the queries it makes to the Amazon server so as to minimize the impact on their servers. Queries are put in a queue and dispatched one at a time, spread out. This means that you might not see the cover art straight away, but it may get download minutes later, when you are already listening to another track.

    Let's leave this bug report open and we'll see if you (and others) can confirm that neither of the things above are happening.
