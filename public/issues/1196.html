Support ticket #1196: Synergy 3.2.1 will not launch automatically at login.

Kind support ticket
Product Synergy
When Created 2009-01-16T13:15:55Z, updated 2009-01-17T19:08:21Z
Status closed
Reporter anonymous
Tags no tags

Description

Synergy 3.2.1 will not launch automatically at login, or when iTunes is launched. I have tried deleting the Synergy preference file, uninstalling and reinstalling Synergy, as well as restarting with no success. I just can't get Synergy to launch at startup, and appear in the menu bar when iTunes launches (per my preferences). I have to keep going into the preference pane to launch it manually. I love Synergy, once it's running, but I really hate having to go in to launch it every time I want to play music. BTW, I'm running OS X 10.5.6 on a Dual 2.3 GHz PowerPC G5 (non Intel).

Comments

  1. Greg Hurrell 2009-01-16T14:15:50Z

    Sounds like you have a stale entry in your Login items.

    Try unchecking the "Launch at login" checkbox, clicking "Apply", then rechecking the checkbox and clicking "Apply".

  2. Greg Hurrell 2009-01-17T19:08:18Z

    I guess that probably fixed it for you. Please add another comment if you'd like to re-open this ticket.
