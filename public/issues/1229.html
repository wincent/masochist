  2. Greg Hurrell 2009-02-16T11:25:38Z

    This usually means you have a stale entry in your login items.

    The usual fix is to go to the Synergy preferences and:

    1. Uncheck the "Launch at login" checkbox
    2. Click "Apply"
    3. Recheck the checkbox
    4. Click "Apply"
