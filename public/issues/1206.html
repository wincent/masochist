Comments

  1. Greg Hurrell 2009-01-25T13:32:09Z

    Most likely you have a stale entry in your login items. The best way to refresh it is to:

    1. Uncheck the "Launch at login" checkbox in the Synergy preferences
    2. Click "Apply"
    3. Recheck the checbox
    4. Click "Apply"

    If that doesn't fix it let me know and I can suggest some other troubleshooting techniques.
