Bug #94: Album Art is only visible in floater after song is switched

Kind bug
Product Synergy
When Created 2005-01-07T18:10:16Z, updated 2007-10-28T05:49:06Z
Status open
Reporter bugzilla user 69
Tags no tags

Description

It will not show the album art for the current song playing in the floater. If the art is downloaded it saves it to the album art folder but will not display it until the song is played again. This is somewhat bothersome and seems to be a new bug in 1.6 that was not seen in 1.4.
