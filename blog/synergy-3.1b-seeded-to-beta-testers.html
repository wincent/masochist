Synergy 3.1b seeded to beta testers

Created 2006-11-13, updated 2026-03-141 minute read

The new version of Synergy, 3.1, is now in beta testing.

The principal new feature in this release is integrated Audioscrobbler (last.fm) support, as well as extensive revisions and optimization of the codebase (see the full changelog for more details).

In the meantime, the previous stable release, version 3.0, continues to be available for download and purchase. Within the next two weeks, an updated beta or a final version of 3.1 will be made available.
