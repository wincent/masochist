Synergy Advance 0.4 beta

Created 2006-03-29, updated 2026-03-141 minute read

Synergy Advance version 0.4 beta has just been released to beta testers. Purchase, donate, sponsor, read the changelog, or download (2.9MB).

Note that this is a limited seed release and that nondisclosure (confidentiality) provisions apply. The build is not for public discussion or redistribution, but membership in the beta program is open to anyone who is interested. I am also happy to provide a complimentary Synergy license on request to any beta tester that purchases a Synergy Advance license.
