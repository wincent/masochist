Server migration coming up in February

Created 2010-01-09, updated 2017-05-181 minute read

Back in November last year I posted about my decision to move from dedicated hosting to "the cloud".

This post is just a little reminder that the move is coming up soon now, in February. I migrated the DNS back in November and the outgoing mail (SMTP) was moved at the same time. More recently I've cancelled my contract with my old host, and am getting serious about preparing things at the AWS end of things.

As with my last big move (from Rackspace to INetU) I'll be doing my best to keep downtime to a minimum (last time it was literally only a few minutes), so hopefully access to everything on the site should be next to uninterrupted.

In the meantime, keep your eye on this space for updates.
