Involuntary Reboot Log 4.3

Created 2013-03-08, updated 2017-05-18

Another instant kernel panic trying to mount an Amazon S3 bucket as a disk using Transmit. This time at the suggestion of Panic support (reinstall then try again).

On a hunch I then switched my standard account from normal user to administrator and tried again. It works.
