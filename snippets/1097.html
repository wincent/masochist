Involuntary reboot log 6.9

Created 2014-12-06, updated 2017-05-18

  • snippets

Previously open browser tab (re-opened with Command-Shift-T) refused to load. Tried in another browser. Tried to quit apps. TextEdit eventually refused to die. Finder started beachballing. Hard reset.

  • Uptime at moment of failure: approximately 2 days
  • Kernel panics: 1
  • Hard resets: 8

  • Total days to date: 318 days
