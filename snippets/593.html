Snippet #593

Created 2010-08-10, updated 2017-05-18
  • snippets

Interarchy 10 is out and boasts "iFTP" as one of its major new features. Apparently this will allow us to "ditch tired old protocols like FTP, WebDAV and SFTP" and "[t]ake your file transfers to the next level". The only other reference I can find to it describes it as "Interarchy's own file transfer protocol built on top of SSH". Funnily enough, I found existing protocols like SFTP to be just fine.
