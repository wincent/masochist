Description

From a user post in the forums:

"I'm using iTunes 6.04, Panther 10.3.9 and Synergy 3 (though it also happened in Synergy 2.9), and whenever I choose the Transfer Cover to iTunes option from the menu bar, Synergy locks up and eventually crashes. I couldn't find anyhting abou this in a quick search. Any ideas?"

http://wincent.com/a/support/forums/showflat.php?Number=1059

My reply:

Thanks for reporting the crash. No one else has reported this issue so can you perform some troubleshooting as recommended here to see if you can rule out other possible causes:

http://wincent.com/s/bugs/

If you can reliably reproduce the crash (and even if you can't) can you please send me a copy of your crash log (stored in ~/Library/Logs/CrashReporter/) and also a copy of the album cover file that you are trying to import (get it by selecting "Show Album Covers folder" from the Synergy Global Menu.

The more information you can provide, the better. For example, what kind of music track are you trying to embed the art in? (An MP3? An AAC-encoded file? etc).

If you don't want to create a Bugzilla account and attach the crash log(s) and the cover art file, you can send them to me via email. My email address is referenced on this page.
