Watir

Created 5/18/2007, updated 5/18/2017
  • watir
  • wiki

Watir or "Web Application Testing in Ruby" is a high-level integration testing tool.

I use it to perform automated in-browser testing of Rails applications. See "Using Watir with RSpec and Rails" for more details.

See also

  • Watir project page: http://wtr.rubyforge.org/
  • Safari-compatible Watir: http://safariwatir.rubyforge.org/
