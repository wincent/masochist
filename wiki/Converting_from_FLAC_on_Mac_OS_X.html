Converting from FLAC on Mac OS XEdit

Created 2006-10-16, updated 2017-05-18

To convert a bunch of FLAC files to WAV:

flac -d *.flac

Requires FLAC to be installed. See Installing PAC (Perl Audio Converter) on Mac OS X.
