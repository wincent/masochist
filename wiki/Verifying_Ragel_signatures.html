Verifying Ragel signaturesEdit

Created 3/26/2008, updated 5/18/2017

In order to verify the signatures on the Ragel source distributions you'll need a copy of Adrian Thurston's public key.

You can get it and import it into GPG as follows:

wget http://www.cs.queensu.ca/home/thurston/thurston.asc
gpg --import thurston.asc

But don't just take my word for it: go to the official Ragel website and Adrian's homepage and confirm the authenticity of the public key for yourself:

  • Official Ragel website: http://www.cs.queensu.ca/home/thurston/ragel/
  • Adrian Thurston's homepage: http://www.cs.queensu.ca/home/thurston/
