Amazon GlacierEdit

Created 2012-11-28, updated 2017-05-18

Amazon Glacier is highly durable, very economical cloud-based data storage for archival purposes. At current prices it's about 12 times cheaper than Amazon's own S3, and just as durable. The trade-off: it's intended for infrequently accessed data, otherwise the charges start mounting up.
