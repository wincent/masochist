Joining images with ImageMagickEdit

2024-05-27

Obtaining ImageMagick

Via Homebrew

brew install imagemagick

Joining vertically

convert -append top.jpg bottom.jpg joined.jpg

Joining horizontally

convert +append left.jpg right.jpg joined.jpg
