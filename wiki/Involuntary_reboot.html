Involuntary rebootEdit

Created 2008-06-12, updated 2017-05-18

An involuntary reboot is a catastrophic system failure such as a hard reset or kernel panic. I keep a log of involuntary reboots for the purposes of measuring system reliability (see "Involuntary Reboot Log").
