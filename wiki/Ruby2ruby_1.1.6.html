Ruby2ruby 1.1.6Edit

Created 2007-06-06, updated 2020-03-12

The new version of ruby2ruby, ruby2ruby 1.1.6, was released on 5 June 2006.

Changes

  • 2 minor enhancements:
    • Extended tests for dstr/dsym/drgx to test against embedded slashes and quotes.
    • Updated for dasgn_curr changes to PT.
  • 2 bug fixes:
    • Fixed a bug with begin/rescue/ensure.
    • Fixed argscat and blockpass bug. blah(42, *args, &block) handled.

See also

  • RubyForge release notes: http://rubyforge.org/frs/shownotes.php?release_id=12193
