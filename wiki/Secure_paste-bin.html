Secure paste-bin

Created 1/22/2014, updated 5/18/2017
  • wiki

I was looking for a "secure" paste-bin with a one-time paste feature (ie. something you could paste to, read from once only, and would then be deleted).

The closest I could find was https://pastee.org/, which allows you to set a short expiry on your pastes (ie. 1 hour), and optionally encrypt them with a passphrase.
