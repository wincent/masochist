last.fmEdit

Created 2009-07-02, updated 2017-05-18

last.fm is a music-based social networking website. It was originally known as "Audioscrobbler" and is still sometimes referred to as such.

Synergy can automatically submit track information to the last.fm website so that your profile gets updated with what you're listening to.
