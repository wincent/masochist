Detaching mis-sized tmux clientsEdit

Created 6/8/2017, updated 7/9/2021

As per my question here, sometimes I need to detach zombie/stale tmux clients that are forcing the view to have a size other the screen on which I am currently using.

This seems to do the trick:

tmux detach-client -a
