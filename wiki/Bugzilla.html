BugzillaEdit

Created 2007-05-11, updated 2024-03-03

Bugzilla is a very popular open source bug tracking system written in Perl. It is both immensely powerful, scalable, customizable, and immensely hard to use for the average non-technical user. For this reason it is particularly popular within hardcore "hacker" communities, but is also the antithesis of "Web 2.0" design.

I used it for several years for tracking bug reports and feature requests.

In April 2008 I switched to a custom issue tracker built on Rails.
