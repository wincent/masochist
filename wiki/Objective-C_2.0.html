Objective-C 2.0

Created 11/16/2007, updated 5/18/2017
  • objective.c
  • wiki

Objective-C 2.0 is the new version of Objective-C that ships with Mac OS X Leopard. Highights include:

  • Garbage collection
  • Properties
  • Fast enumeration (for/in syntax)
