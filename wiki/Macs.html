MacsEdit

Created 2023-11-01, updated 2024-09-19

Here's a list of Macs I've owned or used over the years:

  • Power Macintosh 7600/120
    • MAXpowr G3 500 CPU upgrade
  • PowerBook G3 500 (Firewire/"Pismo")
  • Power Macintosh G4 1.25 DP ("Mirrored Drive Doors")
  • PowerBook G4 1.0 12"
  • Power Macintosh G5 2.5 DP
  • iMac "Core i5" 2.66 27" (Late 2009)
  • iMac "Core Duo" 1.83 17"
  • MacBook Air "Core 2 Duo" 2.13 13" (Late '10) (provided by my employer1)
  • MacBook Air "Core i5" 1.8 13" (Mid-2012) (provided by my employer1)
  • MacBook Air "Core i7" 1.7 13" (Mid-2013) (provided by my employer2, two of them in a row, actually)
  • MacBook Pro "Core i7" 2.8 15" Mid-2015 (Integrated Graphics) (provided by my employer2)
  • MacBook Pro "Core i7" 2.8 15" Mid-2015 (Dual Graphics) (one provided by my employer2, and one I bought for myself)
  • MacBook Pro "Core i7" 2.6 15" Touch/2018 (provided by my employer3)
  • MacBook Pro "M1" 8 CPU/8 GPU 13" (2020) (provided by my employer4)
  • MacBook Pro "M3 Max" 16 CPU/40 GPU 16" (2023)
  • MacBook Pro "M3 Max" 14 CPU/30 GPU 14" (2023) (provided by my employer5)

So, one desktop with a PowerPC 604 CPU (later upgraded to a G3), one G3 laptop, one G4 desktop, one G4 laptop, one G5 desktop, two Intel iMacs, 7 Intel laptops, and 3 Apple Silicon laptops.
