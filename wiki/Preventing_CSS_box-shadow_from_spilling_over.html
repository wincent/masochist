Created 2014-05-16, updated 2017-05-18

Answer here:

  • http://stackoverflow.com/a/5853364/1626737

In short, use spread radius to pull the shadow in, and then adjust the other offset(s) — offset-x and/or offset-y — to compensate and get the desired effect.
