SafariWatirEdit

Created 5/18/2007, updated 5/18/2017

SafariWatir is a port of the Watir project compatible with Apple's Safari browser.

For more information, see "Using Watir with RSpec and Rails" and "Installing SafariWatir on Mac OS X Tiger".
