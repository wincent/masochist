AtomEdit

Created 2008-03-25, updated 2026-03-16

Atom is a pair of web standards for content syndication (feeds) and publishing (creating and updating resources over HTTP). The feeds on this site are delivered in Atom syndication format.

See also

  • Wikipedia page on the Atom standard: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Atom_%28standard%29
  • Official Atom website: http://atomenabled.org/
  • Atom Syndication Format:
    • RFC: RFC 4287
    • Full specification: http://atomenabled.org/developers/syndication/atom-format-spec.php
    • Nice summary: http://www.atomenabled.org/developers/syndication/
  • Atom Publishing Protocol:
    • RFC: RFC 5023
