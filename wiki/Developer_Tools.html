Developer ToolsEdit

Created 2007-03-24, updated 2017-05-18

The "Developer Tools" was the former name of the Xcode Tools. When Apple's free development IDE, Project Builder, was re-written and re-branded as Xcode the Developer Tools themselves changed their name as well (to "Xcode Tools").
