Building mogenerator 1.13.1 on Mac OS Leopard 10.5.6

Created 4/8/2009, updated 5/18/2017
  • mogenerator
  • wiki

Note: mogenerator 1.13.1 isn't actually an official release; 1.13.1 was just the version number at the HEAD of the source repo at the time I made these notes.

Getting the source

mkdir mogenerator
cd mogenerator
git clone git://github.com/rentzsch/mogenerator.git mogenerator.git
svn co http://rentzsch.com/svn/trunk/cocoa/MiscMerge MiscMerge
cd mogenerator.git

Building

Just open the mogenerator.xcodeproj file and click "Build". I expect xcodebuild from the command line would also work.

Installing

mkdir -p ~/Library/Application\ Support/mogenerator
cp templates/*.motemplate ~/Library/Application\ Support/mogenerator/
cp build/Release/mogenerator ~/bin/ # or wherever else you want in your PATH
