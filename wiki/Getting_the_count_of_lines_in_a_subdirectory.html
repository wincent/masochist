Getting the count of lines in a subdirectoryEdit

Created 2014-05-29, updated 2026-03-16

Example: cumulative total:

$ find . -type f -exec cat {} + | wc -l

Example: per file counts and total:

$ find . -regex '.+\.js$' | xargs wc -l
