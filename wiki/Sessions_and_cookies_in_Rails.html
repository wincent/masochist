Sessions and cookies in RailsEdit

Created 2006-07-30, updated 2017-05-18

Do this:

session[:symbol]
cookie[:symbol]

Not this:

@session["symbol"]
@session['symbol']
@session[:symbol]
session["symbol"]
session['symbol']
cookie["symbol"]
cookie['symbol']

In general accessing :symbol will be faster (lightweight class reference) than "symbol" (this has to do string interpolation).
