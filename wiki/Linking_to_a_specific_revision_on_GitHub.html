Linking to a specific revision on GitHubEdit

Created 2014-11-04, updated 2017-05-18

Go to the file, and hit y.

Example

On:

  • https://github.com/wincent/command-t/blob/master/doc/command-t.txt

Hold down shift and click-and-drag over a range of lines; the URL updates to:

  • https://github.com/wincent/command-t/blob/master/doc/command-t.txt#L596-L620

Hit y; the URL updates to:

  • https://github.com/wincent/command-t/blob/9a2653bc91be13b810e63a394574277192927e5d/doc/command-t.txt#L596-L620

Source

  • https://help.github.com/articles/getting-permanent-links-to-files/
