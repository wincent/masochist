Spec::UI 0.2.3Edit

Created 2007-05-24, updated 2017-05-18

Spec::UI 0.2.3 is the new version of Spec::UI, released jointly with RSpec 1.0.1. It was released on 24 May 2007.

Neither the RSpec 1.0.1 nor RSpec 1.0.2 release notes make any specific mention of changes in Spec::UI 0.2.3.
