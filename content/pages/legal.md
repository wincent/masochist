# Privacy Policy

Here's what you need to know about your privacy.

## What I collect

- **Usage logs**: Basic server logs to keep things running smoothly.

## What I don't do

- I don't sell your data to anyone, ever.
- I don't track you across other websites.
- I don't spam you with marketing emails.

## How I protect your data

- I use standard security practices (encryption, secure connections).
- I keep minimal logs and delete old ones regularly.

## When I might share data

- If legally required (court orders, etc).
- With your permission for support requests.

## Your rights

- Contact me with questions or concerns.

## Changes

I'll email you if I make significant changes to this policy.
