---
title: Synergy 3.1.5 released
---

After a successful [beta-testing cycle](http://lists.wincent.com/pipermail/synergy-beta/2007-August/000012.html) the final release version of [Synergy](http://synergy.wincent.com) 3.1.5 is now available for immediate download. This is primarily a maintenance release. A full changelog appears [here](http://www.wincent.com/a/products/synergy-classic/history/#3.1.5).

You can download the new release [from here](http://www.wincent.com/download.php?item=SynergyJaguar.dmg) and purchase a license using [this page](https://secure.wincent.com/a/products/synergy-classic/purchase/). This is a free upgrade for users who already hold a valid Synergy license.
