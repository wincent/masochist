[SafariWatir](/wiki/SafariWatir) is a port of the [Watir](/wiki/Watir) project compatible with [Apple](/wiki/Apple)'s [Safari](/wiki/Safari) browser.

For more information, see "[Using Watir with RSpec and Rails](/wiki/Using_Watir_with_RSpec_and_Rails)" and "[Installing SafariWatir on Mac OS X Tiger](/wiki/Installing_SafariWatir_on_Mac_OS_X_Tiger)".
