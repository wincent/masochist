Alternatives to Google:

- Browser: [Orion] or [Edge]
- Email, calendars, contacts, and notes: [Fastmail]
- File sync: [Resilio Sync]
- Office (spreadsheets, docs etc): [Microsoft Office]/[Microsoft 365]
- Search: [Kagi]
