[WOBase](/wiki/WOBase) was a framework written for use in [Wincent](/wiki/Wincent) products that provides general (base) functionality useful across a wide range of scenarios. In 2006 it was superceded by [WOCommon](/wiki/WOCommon). Examples of shipping products that included the framework are [WinHex](/wiki/WinHex) and [Synergy Advance](/wiki/Synergy_Advance) (prior to version 0.5).
