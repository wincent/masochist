[Spec::UI 0.2.3](/wiki/Spec%3a%3aUI_0.2.3) is the new version of [Spec::UI](/wiki/Spec%3a%3aUI), released jointly with [RSpec 1.0.1](/wiki/RSpec_1.0.1). It was released on 24 May 2007.

Neither the [RSpec 1.0.1](/wiki/RSpec_1.0.1) nor [RSpec 1.0.2](/wiki/RSpec_1.0.2) release notes make any specific mention of changes in [Spec::UI 0.2.3](/wiki/Spec%3a%3aUI_0.2.3).
