A [Frequently Asked Question](/wiki/Frequently_Asked_Question) or [FAQ](/wiki/FAQ) is a either an individual question (or in the case of an [FAQ](/wiki/FAQ) it may be a collection of questions) which crops up frequently with respect to a particular topic. Many use the phrase for any obvious question, even if it is not strictly "frequently asked".
