[RFC 2234](/wiki/RFC_2234) is an [RFC](/wiki/RFC) describing the "Augmented BNF for Syntax Specifications: ABNF".

[ABNF](/wiki/ABNF) is widely used, including in other [RFCs](/wiki/RFCs) (such as [RFC 2822](/wiki/RFC_2822) and [RFC 3986](/wiki/RFC_3986), for example).
