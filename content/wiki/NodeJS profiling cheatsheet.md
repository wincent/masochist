# Function prefixes

-   `^` baseline compiler
-   `*` optimized
-   `~` optimizable

# `LazyCompile`

> does **not** mean that this time was spent in compiler, it just means that the function itself was compiled lazily

[Source](https://stackoverflow.com/a/26834673).
